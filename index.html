About

COGNIT'19

COGNIT'19 is an initiative by the department of Computer Science & Engineering, Sahyadri College of Engineeering & Management, Mangaluru.

It provides a platform for all the young minds to come together to think creatively and solve problems. Students from various colleges and universities will be a part of this event.
